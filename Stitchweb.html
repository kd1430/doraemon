About

Stitch, also known as Experiment 626, is a mischievous and highly intelligent alien created by the evil genius Dr. Jumba in Disney's Lilo & Stitch. Designed as a destructive force, Stitch escapes to Earth, where he accidentally crashes on the Hawaiian islands.
