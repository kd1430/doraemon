About

Doraemon is a robot cat from the future, who travels back in time to help a young boy named Nobita Nobi. Nobita is a kind-hearted, but often lazy and clumsy, student who faces various challenges in his life. Doraemon, sent by Nobita's descendants to improve his life, is equipped with futuristic gadgets from his pocket (called the 4th-dimensional pocket). These gadgets are often used to solve problems and bring about exciting, yet humorous adventures.
